FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance to guarantee availability, especially during high season (May-September). For large events or special dates like weddings, it's better to book 1-2 months in advance. The sooner you contact us, the more options you'll have to secure your ideal date!

What is the service price?

The price varies according to several factors: number of guests, event duration, distance to travel, and menu options. Our packages start from ‚Ç¨14/person for the basic menu, ‚Ç¨19/person for the Temptation menu and ‚Ç¨25/person for the Capital Sin experience. After receiving your request, we'll send you a personalized quote completely without obligation.

What does the FoodTruck service include?

Our service includes everything necessary for a complete GULA experience: the fully equipped FoodTruck, kitchen and service staff, all burgers according to the selected menu, sides, drinks, disposable materials (napkins, containers, etc.), transport and setup/breakdown. We take care of everything so you just have to enjoy the experience with your guests.

Can the menu be customized?

Absolutely! We can adapt our menu to your specific needs, including vegetarian options (like our "Sloth Veggie"), vegan options with Beyond Meat or gluten-free with special bread. We can also create a special themed burger for your event, as we've done with "The Newlyweds' Burger" for weddings or "The Corporate" for business events. Tell us your idea and we'll make it come true.

What technical requirements does the FoodTruck need?

We need a flat space of at least 8x4 meters for the FoodTruck and service area, access for a medium-sized vehicle (our FoodTruck measures 6m long x 2.2m wide x 2.8m high), and preferably a 3500W electrical outlet (although we have a silent generator if this is not possible). We also need permits to access the venue if it's a private space or has restrictions.

What is the service area?

Our FoodTruck operates mainly in Catalonia, based in Terrassa. We can travel up to 150km at no additional cost, covering Barcelona, Girona, Tarragona and their provinces. For longer distances or services in other communities like Madrid, Valencia or Zaragoza, consult with us for a personalized quote that includes travel expenses. We've brought our sinful burgers to all kinds of events throughout Spain!

Is there a minimum number of guests?

Yes, we generally require a minimum of 50 people for standard events. However, for special events like weddings or premium corporate events, we can make exceptions and accept groups of 25-30 people with a personalized budget. For smaller groups, we recommend our catering service where we prepare the burgers at your location without the FoodTruck.

What is the booking and payment process?

The process is very simple: 1) Fill out the booking form on this page; 2) We'll send you a personalized quote within 24h; 3) If you accept it, we request a 30% deposit to confirm the date; 4) One month before the event, we'll hold a final meeting to finalize details; 5) One week before, the remaining 50% is paid, leaving 20% for the day of the event or up to 3 days later. We accept bank transfers, credit cards and Bizum for greater convenience.
